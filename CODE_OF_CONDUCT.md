# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a positive and respectful environment.

Standards
- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Assume good intentions; focus on improvement

Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments
- Trolling or sustained disruption of discussions

Scope
- Applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions) and any public spaces when representing the project

- Report concerns via repository issues (if appropriate) or through GitHubâ€™s reporting tools
- Maintainers may take appropriate action, including removing content or restricting participation
